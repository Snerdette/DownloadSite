Welcome Jake Hsu!

Catalyst's new CEO

As we shared in our last All Hands call, Jake Hsu is joining Catalyst on September 6 as our CEO. For those who have not already looked up Jake’s LinkedIn profile, we thought we’d share a little of his background to help you get to know him.

Jake is a recognized leader in product development and innovation with an international reputation, including serving on boards with the World Economic Forum. For the past nine years, Jake has been leading Symbio, a global software engineering and R&D services company, as their CEO. Under Jake’s leadership, Symbio has grown to become a $600 million leader in product development, digital transformation and innovation.

Like Catalyst, Symbio focuses on developing and delivering innovative products through agile delivery structures and customer-centric design. Jake’s experience at Symbio includes helping companies like Google, Paypal, Uber, Nokia and Microsoft build their core products.

Jake will be based in Baltimore but plans to spend a good deal of time on the West Coast, where his family will continue to live through the 2016 school year.

As Jake joins Catalyst as our CEO, Mike Rosenbaum is moving into the role of Executive Chairman, with a focus on evangelizing Catalyst and our platform for transforming the software engineering market. Mike will serve as a thought leader on the use of data to re-envision the market for software engineering and product development.

These changes are intended to position Catalyst to grow rapidly and extend our leadership role in applying technology to the core of enterprise business models, driving digital transformation and innovation.

Please join us in welcoming Jake!
