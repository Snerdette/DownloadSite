It’s hard to believe that we’re already releasing the second issue of The Download. Kate Slott and team are doing a fantastic job, and I want to thank everyone who has contributed to the publication – we have a lot of great writers at Catalyst! Here’s a quick update on how we’re moving in The Right Direction.

Career
We announced the Q2 promotions during the week of August 1st but in case you missed the email here’s the list. Please join me in congratulating your peers on their promotions →

Reminder: If you haven’t filled out your profile in the Employee Directory and/or haven’t updated your profile in PRaST please take a minute to do so. You can access the tool by clicking here. These tools are used to identify skill sets and staff client projects so it’s critical that your profiles are thorough and kept up to date as you continue to expand your skills.

Culture
We will soon be launching a suggestion box called “What’s on Your Mind.” We are using a 3rd party application that will allow employees to submit anonymously (alternatively you could just shoot Paige an email). This forum will be available for each of you to share ideas, comments, concerns and suggestions for making our company and culture even better. I want to thank Nastassia Pishchykava and Brent Stees for this great idea and for finding the perfect tool that we can use.

There are a lot of fun, social activities in both offices: movie night, game night, poker night, disc golf, Magic the Gathering and an occasional happy hour. Whether you’re working on site or off site, this is a great way to build relationships, relax, and maybe learn a new game. If you’d like to join one/some of these events, but aren’t sure how, reach out to Aimee Alberda in Portland or Paige Cox-Lisk in Baltimore.

Community
Both offices had a successful school supply drive - thank you to everyone for participating. We also have an upcoming Blood Drive in the Beaverton office.
